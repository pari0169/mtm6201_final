Biography

I am a 22-year-old aspiring web designer currently residing in Ottawa. I'm pursuing my studies in Interactive Media Design at Algonquin College, where I'm deeply immersed in learning various facets of digital media. My journey into web design began a few years ago, and since then, l've earned several certificates that highlight my proficiency in this ever-evolving field. My educational background, combined with hands-on experience, has equipped me with a solid foundation in coding and graphic design. I take pride in my ability to create websites that are not only visually appealing but also highly functional and user-friendly.
